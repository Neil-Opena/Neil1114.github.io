About Me

I am currently a 19 year old student at Stony Brook University, pursuing a Bachelor's Degree as a double major in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics/Statistics.

I was born in Baguio City, a city located on the northern-most mainland of the Philippines. However, I grew up here in Long Beach, New York after my parents made the decision to immigrate due to economic reasons.

Prior to attending college, I was never exposed to computer science, but always had a certain favoritism towards math. It was a subject that always challenged and entertained me due to its puzzle-like nature.

Upon taking my first computer science course, I was hooked. Now it has become my goal to actually become a software engineer and use programming as a means to help other people around the world.
