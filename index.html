About ME

Hello! Thank you for taking the time to visit my personal website.
Here's a little bit of information about me.

I am currently a 19 year old student, pursuing a dual Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics/Statistics at Stony Brook University.

I was born in the northern-most mainland of the Philippines, in a city known as Baguio City. We did not have a lot of opportunity there, and eventually my family made the decision to immigrate elsewhere. In 2006, my family and I moved to the United States, and I was ultimately raised in Long Beach, New York.

Growing up, math was the core subject that fueled my interest in problem solving. It was a subject that I understood naturally, no matter how difficult the concepts were. It was also the only subject that kept me awake during school. I was never really exposed to programming and Computer Science, until I attended my first year of college. However, upon taking the introductory course and seeing my first line of code appear on a computer, I knew that I made the perfect choice for a major. A major that could have unlimited potential in making a difference in people's lives.

In the end, my goal is to not only help people, but to make a lasting impact. Though, I know that it may be far into the future, I am willling to do whatever it takes to get there.
